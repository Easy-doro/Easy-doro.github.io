Edison and the Frog

[Work in progress]

Pitch :

You play as a clumsy bipedal frog who is forced to eat fireflies to power the light bulb on his forehead, his journey companion Edison. Someday, a spaceship crashes on your planet, its passengers plunge your planet into darkness by capturing fireflies, a precious fuel to charge their ship. You'll have to fight the aliens and your fear of the dark to move forward and save the fireflies.
